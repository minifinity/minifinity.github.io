As a computer science student, Vim is bound to sneak through conversations here and there. I first thought this was a programming language, then an IDE (some argue that it is; but I was thinking VSCode). Then I learned that it is just... a text editor.

Perhaps that was why I put off learning it for so embarrassingly long. Why would you need a whole tool to add and delete some letters here and there? Just take your mouse, move the cursor to wherever, add/delete, and move on. Or ChatGPT, ctrl-A, ctrl-C, ctrl-V, essay done.
